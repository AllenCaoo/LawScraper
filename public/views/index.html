Welcome to LawScraper

Made by Allen Cao

The passage of laws plays an important role in everyday life. Decisions on civil rights, the econonmy, and treaties are impactful.

Unfortunately, citizens often do not ever hear about new laws being passed and the effects it will have on their lives. Even if they do, it is usually through the news, which sometimes have different interpretations of the laws.

LawScraper provides people with raw information about these laws - laws and their respective summaries is gathered directly from government websites. The readers have the chance to conveniently interpret laws for themselves and compare those interpretations with those on the news.

Everyday, LawScraper will scrape the government websites for new laws and email our mailing list a summary of these new laws (again directly from the government websites).
